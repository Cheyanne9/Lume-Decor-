About Lumé Décor

Personalized glowing décor made to reflect your personality.

Our Story

What began as a small creative dream has grown into a decor studio devoted to design pieces that don't just decorate your space but to bring your imagination to life. Each creation of custom neon signs to personalized mirrors and artistic lighting is crafted with intention, emotion and personality. We belive that decor is more than style. It's more like memory, expression, mood and the way your home smiles back at you when you walk in. At Lume, our mission is simple. We are here to inspire warmth and creativity to every room. We combine different design with high quality materials to bring your dream or vision to reality whether it is your bedroom, a cozy corner, business or as a gift.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To transform simple spaces into personal inspiring environment and to deliver high quality customizable décor that empowers self expression through interior design.

Vision: To become Jamaica’s go to small brand for creative, affordable, and glowing custom décor.
